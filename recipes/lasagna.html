Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that combines layers of pasta, seasoned ground beef, tomato sauce, and a mix of cheeses to create an unforgettable culinary experience. To prepare it, first, saut√© the ground beef with onion, garlic, and spices like oregano and basil, then mix it with a rich tomato sauce. In a baking dish, alternate layers of pre-cooked pasta with the meat and sauce mixture, along with generous layers of ricotta and mozzarella cheese, finishing with a top layer of grated Parmesan cheese.

Once assembled, the lasagna is baked until the cheese layers melt and lightly brown on top, while the flavors blend and intensify. After removing it from the oven, let it rest for a few minutes to allow the layers to set, then cut into perfect portions for serving. This traditional dish, with its combination of textures and flavors, is ideal for family dinners and special gatherings, and is sure to be a hit among all diners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
